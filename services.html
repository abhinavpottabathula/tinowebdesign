TECNO

An Electrifying Experience

Looking back, many timeless classics have become antiquated games. On the other hand, people constantly seek technology of the future. Tecno aims to reconcile this difference, providing facilities that bring entertainment of the past and future to the present. Here is a short description of our premium services:

Special Events

Every month, Tecno hosts special events for our guests. Look out for:

  • Happy Hour (Drinks are half price)
  • Toddler Thursdays (kids ages 7 and younger play for free)
  • Family Fiesta (50% for families of 4 or more)
  • Game Day Gatherings (Parties of 8+ get a special discount)

Kids' Korner

<

>

Kids love to run. That’s no secret. But sometimes it can be hard for these children to be overseen… until Tecno introduced the Kids Korner. This giant room is a dream come true for children! With play structures to climb, trampolines and ball pits to jump in, and an obstacle course to run through, we take partying to the next level. And when the kids get tired, they can take a break at the mini arcade full of timeless classics and the latest hits! Our chaperones ensure that the kids have fun and stay safe, so you can be rest assured that your kids will be well taken care of. And as an added bonus, kids get to leave with a prize in hand! As they play, they get to collect tickets to claim prizes at our Souvenir Center.

>

Teen Territory

Teenagers of today are glued to their mobile devices, much to the disappointment of their parents. But Tecno plans on solving this issue; with our incorporation of arcades, Virtual Reality  and 4D simulations, we turn a teen’s night on Youtube into an event of entertainment and elation. We’ve also added other activities. Our most popular game, the Room Escape, is a series of puzzles that teenagers must solve to escape. Combined with our theatre, dance floor and video game center, the Teen Territory can revolutionize the way teens party.

Adult Area

<

>

Over the years, family entertainment has evolved into a wearisome chore for parents. And lackluster office dinners have taken over lively celebrations. Tecno aims to fix that! With an exciting, yet mature atmosphere, our Adult Area hosts a billiards arena, a bar and lounge. Rejuvenate your party with other small games like bingo, piñatas and scavenger hunts. Step away from dull parties and enjoy a fabulous night at Tecno’s Adult Area.

>

Karaoke & Dance Floor

Food. Games. Guests. Here at Tecno, that’s not all that makes a party. After all, what is a party without music and dance? One of our most popular features is the karaoke and dance floor. Make a playlist of songs (or use some of ours!) and hit the dance floor with your friends!

Power Points

What’s better than playing at the arcade? Playing to earn prizes! As you win games at the arcade, you earn power points. Each point can then be spent at our Souvenir Center, to choose from hundreds of prizes for free! And even better, save up these points across multiple visits to win bigger and better prizes. 200 points gets you a t-shirt or hat, 1000 points gets you a gaming device, and 2000 points gets you the ultimate prizes: a VR headset or new pair of headphones!
